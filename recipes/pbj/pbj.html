Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Quick and Easy Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich!

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  3. Close both slices together with peanut butter and jelly on the inside
  4. Cut in half
