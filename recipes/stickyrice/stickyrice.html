Sticky Rice

Quick and Easy Sticky Rice - no rice cooker needed!

Ingredients

  • Medium-sized pot
  • 2 Cups white rice (short grain or calrose)
  • 2 Cups water

Steps

  1. Rinse and drain rice 7 times
  2. Add water to pot and set heat on high (until boil)
  3. Once boiling, add rice into pot and set heat to low
  4. Cook for 10-minutes
  5. After 10-minutes, turn off burner and let rice sit for 5-minutes
